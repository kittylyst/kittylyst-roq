In general, I regard myself as "on-the-fence" regarding AI coding tools. I can see benefit in the tools, in a number of use cases, and both the capability and the range of applicability is both improving. However, to date, I have not personally encountered the transformative "end of human coding" experience that some others experience. I still encounter fairly hard limitations on a cadence between weekly and daily.

My primary tools are Perplexity and Cursor.

I use Cursor on the default settings. This is quite deliberate, as I am interested in the mass-market experience, not the enthusiast / hobbyist case.
